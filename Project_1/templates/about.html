About Us

TheBus is the only public bus transportation service on the island of Oʻahu, Hawai'i.

Founded in 1971, we are privately managed by the nonprofit Oahu Transit Services Inc., which operates the system under a public-private partnership with the City and County of Honolulu's Department of Transportation Services. In 2024, TheBus had:
